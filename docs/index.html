FreeLingo

Open source, self-hosted language learning with AI.
A local LLM evaluates your CEFR level, builds a personalized study plan, and guides you through lessons, spaced-repetition flashcards, AI tutor chat, voice conversation, listening exercises, reading practice, and a full learning resources hub.

Features

Everything you need to learn a language

🎯

CEFR Placement Test

Adaptive quiz determines your A1–C2 level automatically before building your plan.

📚

Personalized Study Plan

Curriculum-driven weekly roadmap based on your level and schedule intensity (4–16 weeks).

✍️

AI-Generated Lessons

Grammar, vocabulary, reading, and writing lessons generated within strict CEFR boundaries.

🃏

Spaced Repetition Flashcards

SM-2 algorithm schedules reviews at exactly the right time. Cards include native-language translations.

💬

AI Tutor Chat

Streaming SSE tutor with real-time feedback, fully aware of your current unit and progress.

🎙️

Voice Conversation

Real-time voice pipeline with TTS/STT, VAD, and barge-in for live speaking practice with the AI tutor.

📖

Learning Resources Hub

Grammar reference, vocabulary hub, phrasebook, and skills tracker — all in one place.

🎧

Listening & Reading Exercises

AI-generated audio and reading comprehension exercises to train your skills at your CEFR level.

🔒

Privacy First & Any LLM

100% self-hosted with Ollama, OpenAI, Anthropic, or DeepSeek. Your data, your server, your rules.
