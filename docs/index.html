AI language learning platform

Learn with Lingu, your AI language tutor.

FreeLingo combines CEFR placement, personalized study plans, multi-language progress, flashcards, voice conversation, listening and reading exercises, and native-language learning support.

Try FreeLingo How it works

Features

Everything in one structured learning loop.

01

Placement and study plans

Start with a CEFR assessment, then get a weekly roadmap adapted to your level, target language, and pace.

02

AI tutor chat and memory

Lingu streams feedback in context, remembers useful learner details, and keeps conversations tied to your plan.

03

Voice conversations

Practice speaking with TTS/STT, voice activity detection, barge-in, persisted transcripts, and a post-assessment demo.

04

Listening and reading

Generate level-aware audio and reading comprehension exercises that match your current study language.

05

Resources and flashcards

Use grammar, vocabulary, phrasebook, skills tracking, and SM-2 flashcards with native-language translations.

06

Multi-language progress

Study English, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, German, French, Japanese, Korean, and Mainland Chinese side by side.
